# Privacy Policy - TeachAssist Desktop

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

TeachAssist Desktop is a grade tracking application for YRDSB (York Region District School Board) students. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect

TeachAssist Desktop **does not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

### What the app does with your data:
- Your YRDSB student number and password are used **only** to authenticate with the YRDSB TeachAssist website.
- Credentials are stored **locally** on your device using Windows Credential Manager.
- Grade data is cached **locally** for offline viewing.
- No data is ever sent to any third-party server.

### What the app does NOT do:
- We do not collect personal information.
- We do not use analytics or tracking.
- We do not share data with third parties.
- We do not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or other device features.

## Network Access

The app connects only to YRDSB servers (`teachassist.yrdsb.edu.on.ca`) to retrieve your grades. This requires the `internetClient` capability.

## Your Control

- You can clear all cached data at any time from Settings > Clear Cached Data.
- You can delete your stored credentials from Settings > Logout.
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app's Store listing.
